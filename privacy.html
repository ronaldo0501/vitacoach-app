VitaCoach Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

VitaCoach ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use the VitaCoach iOS app.

Information We Collect

VitaCoach collects health and wellness data you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Shift schedules and work patterns
  • Sleep duration and quality ratings
  • Daily wellness check-in data (energy, mood, stress, hydration)
  • Exercise and nutrition information
  • Habit tracking data

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide personalized AI health coaching recommendations tailored to your shift work schedule. We use the Anthropic Claude API to generate coaching responses. Data sent to the API is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.

Data Storage

Your health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and optionally synced across your devices via iCloud. We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal health data to third parties beyond what is necessary to provide AI coaching responses.

Subscriptions

Subscription and payment management is handled by RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. Payment information is processed exclusively by Apple and is never stored by us.

Children's Privacy

VitaCoach is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
